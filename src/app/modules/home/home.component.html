ZUN inspired music page

I like ZUN's music. He makes good music. Fortunately for fans of his work, many independent music groups have reworked and remixed his music. So many derivatives have been made in fact, that finding and organizing them has become a gigantic pain in the ass. This is further exacerbated by the fact that these groups are pre-dominantly Japanese who do not speak a lick of English.
Trying to organize your bookmarks of arcane squiggly lines is no fun, I tell you that. This page is just my personal way of dealing with this shit. I get youtube videos of a specific artist or group's work, then paste them in their corresponding tab.
